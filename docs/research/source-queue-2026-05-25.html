2. Validation Ladder

Lead

Interesting mention, video, post, or claim. Not trusted yet.

Traceable

Has DOI, PMID, trial ID, guideline, named lab, or clinician source.

Relevant

Matches Eric's condition or a clearly labeled adjacent mechanism.

Actionable

Changes protocol, metric, hardware, clinician question, or research watchlist.

Parked

Too speculative, commercial, generic, or not tied to hemianopia/stroke vision rehab.

6. What Gets Promoted To The Wiki

Promotion rule
A source queue item should enter `.brain/wiki/` only if it changes a stable fact, evidence confidence, protocol choice, measurement plan, hardware decision, or clinician question list.
Finding Type Destination

Verified paper or guideline `.brain/wiki/research-papers-index.md` plus relevant topic page.
