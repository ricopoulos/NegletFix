Outcome

What changed

Before this session, outside research was useful but scattered. After this session, NegletFix has a repeatable method: capture leads from YouTube or X, extract claims, validate against papers/guidelines/trials, then either promote, watch, park, or reject.

Decision rules

The validation ladder

Promote

A lead becomes NegletFix knowledge only when supported by a peer-reviewed paper, guideline, clinical trial registry, reputable medical organization, or Eric's own measurement data.

Watch

Interesting but incomplete topics stay on watchlists: QEEG/tPBM, medication adjuncts, active trials, named researchers, or technologies that need replication.

Park or reject

Anecdotes, miracle claims, commercial protocols, and generic neuroplasticity language do not change the Unity protocol or the clinical plan.

Clinical boundary

What should not change yet

Unity protocol

The audiovisual training plan remains the main path. The research sprints did not create a reason to change WIP-001 before the Editor smoke test.

QEEG and Muse

QEEG is clinical EEG analysis, not a magic target map. Muse remains an exploratory feedback layer, not a clinical QEEG system.

Medication

Medication is a physician conversation only. The most useful question is whether any current or new medication can safely improve training tolerance by addressing fatigue, sleep, mood, or side effects.

Doctor conversation

Medication takeaways

Fluoxetine

Only direct visual-field drug lead, but FLUORESCE was tiny and acute-only. Large fluoxetine stroke-recovery evidence does not support routine use for recovery.

Ask as literature, not request
Levodopa

Older motor-stroke rationale is now weakened by DARS and ESTREL. No direct chronic-HH evidence found.

Low priority
Modafinil

Possibly relevant only if neuro-fatigue blocks training. It is not a visual recovery drug.

Symptom support only
Methylphenidate / rotigotine

Only makes sense if a clinician confirms neglect or attention/arousal impairment distinct from HH.

Requires diagnosis
